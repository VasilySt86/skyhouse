  • Things to Do in Bali
  • Things to Do in Villa

Villa Sky House's southern location puts it in easy reach of many of Bali's best facilities and attractions. Within 20 minutes are two championship golf courses, water sport centers, many fine restaurants, five star resorts, countless spas and Bali's best beaches at Jimbaran, Balangan and Nusa Dua.

Attractions located less than 30 minutes driving distance from the villa:

— A secret virgin beach of Bali is located only 7 minutes away from the villa. Locals Balinese and a few tourists know of this little paradise. There is a local cafe where you can purchase ice-cold beer and also rent sun loungers and umbrellas.
— Bali's best beaches: Jimbaran, Nusa Dua, Balangan, Dreamland, Padang Padang.
— Championship golf courses: New Kuta Golf Course and Bali Golf and Country Club.
— Water sports in Tanjung Benoa (Nusa Dua).
— Waterbom aquapark in Kuta.
— Uluwatu temple.
— Uluwatu beach with its many simple surfer warungs (local eateries). Perfect for a cold sunset beer. Newly opened "Single Fin" bar and lounge is a nice place with chill out atmosphere.
— Many of Bali's top five-star hotels and restaurants and spas.
— Bali's most famous surf breaks including Kuta, Uluwatu and the Impossible Beach.
— The Thalasso therapy centre in the Ayana Resort (ex Ritz-Carlton Bali).
— Jimbaran Beach's world famous seafood BBQ warungs (restaurants).

For excursions out to the greater Bali region like a trip to the Elephant Park or shopping in the galleries and craft workshops in the cultural centre of Ubud, or even some dare-devil adventure activities such as white water rafting or climbing a volcano for sunrise, our staff will be more than happy to offer advice and arrange everything for you. All you have to do is sit back and enjoy your holiday.

Try in-villa SPA treatments from our friends Div SPA. This family SPA running by Balinese family pak (Mr) Ketut and ibu (Mrs) Nyoman. Nyoman is formerly Bvlgari hotel's SPA resident and she is also hereditary healer. Don't hesitate to ask our butler Eddy to make an appointment with Ibu Nyoman if you feel any pain or problems with your bones or muscles.
They can also send car to pick up you from villa at any time and drop to their village style SPA with lush garden and traditional SPA pavilions. For mental and relaxation treatments we especially recommend you to go to the SPA that is only in 10 minutes driving distance from the Sky House where Ibu Nyoman and her staff can be more concentrated for better results.
For in-house treatments SPA provides portable massage beds, batik sarongs and bathrobes then you can feel comfortable staying in air conditioned bedroom or being in the roof top Sky Lounge (roof top treatments in day time are not recommended due to strong tropical sun, therapist need shady place also).

Order your car to pick up you early in the morning and go to the Jimbaran traditional fish market. This is one the place in Bali must to see. Wide selection of last night catched fish and underwater creatures available from iceboxes. Prices are very reasonable.
You can choose whatever you like to cook by yourself or simply invite one of management's company professional chefs to prepare your favorite seafood in BBQ style or just pan fried with garlic and some exotic spices. Chef's fee is 350.000 IDR (about 40 USD) per event. Please note to provide tips for villa staff about 100.000 IDR / hour for both if you like them to serve you until late night.

Take a walking trek over hilly landscapes that surround villa Sky House. Bypass villa from the right to go to the traditional Balinese village with local temples. Take straight ahead to come near by Garuda Wisnu Kencana Park's with giant statues of Wisnu and Garuda the firebird. There are some places here with great views over the Jimbaran bay. Take right by the road and go down the hill to reach sacred Goa Gong temple in a cave. Please don't forget about local rules and regulations when you are entering the Balinese temples. Villa staff will be happy to consult or accompany you.

Stay full day in roof top bale gazebo, writing novel or notes for your diary about your Asian holidays. Bale is located about 120 meters above the sea level and always cooled by the ocean breeze. It has also mosquito net and you can even sleep in this place. We also placed sound system with iPod connection and excellent collection of lounge music. Or just forget about laptop and spend your day leaf thru design books from villa's library.

Watch HD movie accompanied by hi-end surround sound system in villa's entertainment room. We have latest shot of Bondiana, James Cameron's the best Avatar, Iron Man, Rambo and many others on Blu-ray disks. Feel like in real cinema in a privacy of your villa.

All of our recommendation based on owner's experience. We can not guarantee you will satisfy or not if you will follow.
