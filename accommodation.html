  • Accommodation

Villa Sky House can easily accommodate up to six people but due to the villa's layout, we recommend it for one couple in the master suite and one couple with two children who will stay in very comfortable family suite which consist of bedroom and entertainment room with fold out sofa. Extra bed and baby cot are also available on request - one baby cot and one baby high chair are free of charge.

Master Suite

This is an oversized room with floor to ceiling French doors, traditional wooden high pitched ceilings and laminated flooring.

Master suite amenities:

  • — Extra king size master bed
  • — Bedside tables and lamps
  • — Spacious walk-in wardrobe with a mirror
  • — Very big and very bright en-suite bathroom with wall mirror, toilet, two wash basins, bathtub for two and rain shower in front of sliding windows that can be opened to the views over surrounding landscape and the distant volcanic peaks of Bali
  • — A 46" flat screen TV with satellite TV channels and home theatre sound system with DVD player
  • — Balcony with rattan sofa set for two
  • — Soft armchair with ottoman, coffee table and reading lamp
  • — Connected study room with picture windows

Family Suite

Is an open-plan suite with shared entrance, bathroom and balcony, and suitable for a family with two kids. Parents sleep in a king-sized bed while the kids sleep in the entertainment / media room on a pull-out sofa.

  • — King size bed
  • — Wardrobe
  • — Spacious bathroom with toilet, wash basin, big bathtub and rain shower
  • — Bed side tables and lamps

The entertainment or media room is one of the most interesting places in the villa. It has the hi-definition TV and the hi-end sound system, the library with good selection of books and some design albums as well. There are also bins with International magazines in every bedroom. It's furnished with individually designed teak wood coffee table, TV console and bookcase which is the restored antique gem from Java. Finally we placed comfortable beanbags for wonderful home cinema experience all together.

We put very comfortable King Koil mattresses on each bed in the master suite and family bedroom and use only high-class hotel quality natural cotton linen and towels, and provide two pillows for every guest.

In the bathrooms we supply bathrobes, soft towels, hair dryers and all toiletries. Our shampoo, shower gel and bath foams were specially created for Villa Sky House. They are fresh and with exotic flower accents.

There is safety deposit box installed in every wardrobe.

Villa Sky House

Sky House is an architecturally minimalist yet elegantly designed luxury villa tucked into the contours of a scenic hillside on the highest point of the Bukit peninsula in Bali, only a stone's throw from the island's most beautiful beaches. It is also blessed with the most outstanding wide angle views in Bali, stretching all the way off into the distant horizon and filled with beaches, towering volcanoes, offshore islands and the rich fertile plains of southern Bali.
