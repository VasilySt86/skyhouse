Villa Sky House is a nice and cozy family house, tastefully decorated and furnished by the owners and comes fully-equipped with everything you need for both everyday living or a holiday vacation.

First of all, special thanks go out to our valuable Sky House crew: the butler, the housekeeping and maintenance, the villa manager and members of security team for making every guest's stay memorable and full of positive emotions thanks to their smiles and caring and respectful attitude.

The following services are included in the rental rate to let you concentrate on your holiday and family time with no worries about routine.

Complimentary Services

  • — Daily housekeeping
  • — Hotel quality cotton linen, soft towels and bath robes which are changed daily

  • — Full time butler service for your daily needs - from grocery shopping to tours arrangements

Guest Arrival

We welcome every guest with chilled drinks and cold towels and your fridge will be packed with soft drinks, mineral water, a selection of fruits and freshly made sandwiches.
We also provide a complimentary selection of teas include black, green and jasmine teas from the best plantations in Java, as well as selected arabica coffee blends or coffee from Bali

Room Service

In conjunction with the nearby Beverly Hills Bali Resort we offer a full room service menu. Order whatever you like from the breakfast, lunch or dinner menus and it will be delivered fresh to the villa. Last order is at 9:30pm.
We also have a selection of menus from other restaurants including Indonesian, seafood, Thai and Chinese cuisines, which will be delivered or picked up by your butler.

Car with Driver

We provide a seven-seater Toyota Avanza with English-speaking driver for eight hours a day up till 11pm (excluding petrol, parking and toll fees). Extra time and usage after 11pm will be charged at the low rate about USD 5-6 / hour in Indonesian Rupiah. You can upgrade your car to bigger size mini-van for a nominal fee.

Breakfast

Breakfast is not included but can be prepared in your villa by our butler. We have a limited menu onsite so should you require anything more substantial feel free to order whatever you like for lunch or dinner, and our staff will head out to the supermarket and get it for you at cost price.

Spa

For in-house spa treatments we recommend very the professional therapists from our friends, DIV SPA. This family-run spa is owned by Pak (Mr) Ketut and Ibu (Mrs) Nyoman. Ketut is formerly Bvlgari hotel's spa resident and she is also a hereditary healer. Don't hesitate to ask your butler Eddy to make an appointment with Ibu Nyoman if you feel any pain or problems with your bones or muscles. She will be more than happy to help.

SPA Menu

Villa Sky House

Sky House is an architecturally minimalist yet elegantly designed luxury villa tucked into the contours of a scenic hillside on the highest point of the Bukit peninsula in Bali, only a stone's throw from the island's most beautiful beaches. It is also blessed with the most outstanding wide angle views in Bali, stretching all the way off into the distant horizon and filled with beaches, towering volcanoes, offshore islands and the rich fertile plains of southern Bali.
