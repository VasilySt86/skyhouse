Villa Sky House is located at the highest point of the Bukit Peninsula in south Bali, close to the popular tourist areas of Jimbaran and Nusa Dua.

You can read our short entertainment guide about what to do and where to go in the Bukit to get some ideas about what to do on your holiday.

  • Beautiful white sand beach 7 minutes
  • Denpasar Ngurah Rai International Airport 20 minutes
  • Jimbaran Beach 10 minutes
  • Balangan Beach 15 minutes
  • Nusa Dua Beach 25 minutes
  • New Kuta Golf Course in Pecatu (Dreamland) 15 minutes
  • Uluwatu temple and Uluwatu Beach 25 minutes
  • Bali Collection shopping mall in Nusa Dua 20 minutes
  • Waterbom park and Discovery Mall in Kuta 30 minutes
  • Kuta's nightlife 30 minutes
  • Seminyak restaurants 45 minutes

Villa Sky House

Sky House is an architecturally minimalist yet elegantly designed luxury villa tucked into the contours of a scenic hillside on the highest point of the Bukit peninsula in Bali, only a stone's throw from the island's most beautiful beaches. It is also blessed with the most outstanding wide angle views in Bali, stretching all the way off into the distant horizon and filled with beaches, towering volcanoes, offshore islands and the rich fertile plains of southern Bali.
